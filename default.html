Additional Details

We provide free access to the Makalani Bird & Tea Garden situated at the Makalani Holiday Resort.

Take the opportunity to walk around the park's peaceful surroundings while looking at the bright plumage of the various species available. We provide a safe haven for all orphaned and injured aves & mammals.

For an additional fee, be bold and enter the Bird of Prey section for a guided tour. Watch a free-flying bird show at 15h00pm! (Weekends and public holidays only)
