Hartebeespoort Aquarium

The only fresh water fish Aquarium in South Africa

Established in 1962, the Hartebeespoort Aquarium boasts a variety of both indigenous and tropical freshwater fish.

We have an "education through conservation" policy where we

  • educate the public and
  • provide a safe haven for orphaned and injured aquatic animals.

We house aquatic animals like the Cape Fur Seal, Great White Pelicans and Nile Crocodiles, just to mention a few.

Free access to Makalani Bird & Tea Garden

Your entry fee for the aquarium also gets you free entry into our Bird & Tea Garden situated at the Makalani Holiday Resort.

Take the opportunity to walk around the parks peaceful surroundings while looking at the bright plumage of the various species available. We provide a safe haven for all orphaned and injured aves & mammals.

For an additional fee be bold and enter the Bird of Prey section for a guided tour. Watch a free-flying bird show at 15h00pm! (Week-ends and public holidays only)
